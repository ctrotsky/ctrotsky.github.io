About

I am a student of Computer Science at Cal Poly Pomona with a passion for software and game development. I'm an avid programmer with over 5 years of experience in game development with technologies such as Unity and GameMaker Studio. I have worked in a gameplay programming position on a number of projects for hackathons and game jams, as well as larger scale projects.

My accomplishments in game development include winning 'Best Game Hack' and '2nd Place Overall' at Hackpoly 2017 at Cal Poly Pomona, as well as winning 'Best Game Hack' at Citrushack 2016 at UC Riverside.

I am currently earning my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science from Cal Poly Pomona, and will be graduating in June of 2017.

InVRasion

Previous Next

InVRasion is a work in progress virtual reality game for the HTC VIVE. It is an asynchronous multiplayer game in which a single VR player competes against up to four other players who use co-operative splitscreen. The VR player plays as a giant, city destroying monster, while the other players play as soldiers who attempt to defeat the monster before it destroys an objective.

InVRasion has been in development since January of 2017 as a Senior Project for my degree in Computer Science at Cal Poly Pomona. After receiving academic credit for InVRasion, development has continued as a hobby since March of 2017.

Created using Unity3D Game Engine and C#.

Note: Requires an HTC VIVE to play.


  • Date: March 2017

Battle Of The Bards

Previous Next

A clash between Rhythm and RPG genres, this game lets you fight an RPG boss battle with the power of rock! Players fight a monster by using spell cards which are cast by playing rhythm game segments using a guitar controller or keyboard. Their song performance affects the strength of the spell.

Battle Of The Bards was created in just 24 hours at the Hackpoly 2017 hackathon, and won 'Best Game Hack' and '2nd Place Overall'.

Created using Unity3D Game Engine and C#.

Note: Supports USB Guitar Hero or Rock Band controllers as well as keyboard controls.


  • Project Site: Battle Of The Bards | HackPoly 2017 Devpost

  • Play in browser: Battle Of The Bards

  • Date: January 2017

Seismic Shift

Previous Next

An Infinite Runner game themed on an earthquake destroying a city. Players drive a car through procedurally generated city roads attempting to survive. Seismic waves propagate down the roads, throwing everything in its path into the air.

Seismic Shift was created in just 36 hours at Global Game Jam 2017 at UC Irvine. The theme of the game jam for this year was 'Waves'.

Created using Unity3D Game Engine and C#.


  • Project Site: Seismic Shift | Global Game Jam 2017

  • Play in browser: SeismicShift

  • Date: January 2017

Sumo Pucks

Previous Next

Sumo Pucks is a competitive multiplayer party game for 2 to 4 players. Players attempt to bounce each other off a platform, and the last one standing wins. Features a variety of power-ups and loads of couch-multiplayer fun.

Sumo Pucks was created in just 48 hours at Citrus Hack 2016 hackathon, and won 'Best Game Hack' .

Created using Unity3D Game Engine and C#.



Note: Requires at least 2 players and at least 1 gamepad to play.
